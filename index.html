Comrades of whodyhwut-now?

We're a musical artist collective

I'm Cassie Candles. I had this idea of making a network of songwriter/producer friends to form a collective. It's pretty new rn, but eventually, when there's enough of us, I'd like to put together a compilation CD with a few songs from each of us, and eventually host festival :) It would be the annual "Comrades of Cassie Candles Collective Compilation CD" and the annual, "Comrades of Cassie Candles Celebration"

I had a dream the other night i was managing an artist collective. I've heard of artist collectives before. There's one in Seattle called 'Enby Party' that I'm somewhat envious of. It's a bunch of trans and non-binary musicians that have an annual festival and compilation tape, and connect songwriters to producers and the like. I was envious because there aren't enough people in my town to have something like that, then I had this dream.

I woke up like, "wait, what would I need to do to have an artist collective? And then I realized it's probably a lot easier than it seems. So This one's still pretty early in the making, but once I get at least five members, I'll put out our first compilation CD! Hopefully it will grow from there to the point we can plan a festival X3

It's funny, I told my girlfriend about the idea and she was like, "if you open up a mutual fund for the compilation CD it'd be, 'Comrades of Cassie Candles Collective Compilation CD CD', and if you make exclusively for cross-dressers, it becomes, 'Comrades of Cassie Candles Collective Compilation CD CD CD'". Please let me know if there are more opportunities for alliteration <3
